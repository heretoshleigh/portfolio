Getting started is easier than you think

Web development doesn't have to be an overwhelming or expensive process. I specialize in getting individuals, small businesses, and nonprofits up and running with simple, compelling websites designed to help them reach their goals. If you'd like to explore a partnership, the process is straightforward:

Consultation

Let's talk! Tell me about yourself, your goals, and your vision for your site, and I'll tell you how I can help. Consultations are always free.

Proposal

I'll develop a project proposal (also free) detailing how we can reach your goals within your budget. If you like what you see, we'll sign a contract.

Development

With frequent input from you, I'll design, build, and test your new site. You'll get weekly progress updates, so you'll always know where we stand.

Launch

We'll take your shiny new website live! If you want me by your side for ongoing support, I'm available, but a retainer is never required.

About me

I'm a Denver-based software engineer, focused on full-stack web development.

An analytical thinker by nature, I'm always looking for a problem to solve or a system to improve - the gnarlier, the better. My work is driven by big-picture goals, while attentive to all the tiny details that put those goals within reach.

Equity and empathy are two of my most deeply held values, and it shows in my work.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking (I once walked from Mexico to Canada for fun!), mushroom foraging, dancing at electronic shows, traveling to places I've never been, and eating my way through Denver's best restaurants with my husband and close friends.
